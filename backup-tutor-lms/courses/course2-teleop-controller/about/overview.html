TeleOp Controller Certification

Learn to control robots. Get certified. Get hired.

This course teaches you everything you need to operate robots on the MarsGeo platform — from logging in for the first time to completing paid jobs on the TeleOp Hub. No technical background required.

What you'll be able to do

  • Connect to and control any robot on the platform
  • Use drive controls, arm controls, and voice commands
  • Monitor your robot using live video and telemetry
  • Record sessions that become AI training data
  • Work safely and produce high-quality demonstrations
  • Find and complete paid teleoperation jobs

Who this is for

Anyone who wants to operate robots — no coding, no engineering degree, no AI knowledge needed.

Duration

10 modules · ~4 hours · Self-paced · Certificate on completion
