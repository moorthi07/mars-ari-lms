## Course 1 — Physical AI Foundations
**Course Code:** PAI101  
**Duration:** 8 modules | ~6 hours  
**Tone:** Technical/Mix  
**Audience:** Engineers, ML practitioners, builders, students  
**Status:** ✅ Module 1-2 content written

### Modules
1. ✅ What Is Physical AI — *Classical vs learned, VLM vs VLA, data bottleneck*
2. ✅ Robot Fundamentals — *State/action spaces, DOF, sensors, control frequency*
3. ⏳ Simulation Environments — *MuJoCo/Gazebo/Isaac, robot models, control loop*
4. ⏳ Teleoperation — *Human-in-loop, interfaces, episode structure, data quality*
5. ⏳ Dataset Engineering — *Schema, sync, storage, cleaning, distribution shift*
6. ⏳ Policy Learning — *Behavior cloning, MLP/CNN, training loop, evaluation*
7. ⏳ VLM & VLA Models — *Visual grounding, language-conditioned policies, OpenVLA*
8. ⏳ Sim-to-Real & The Full Loop — *Domain randomization, safety, iteration*

## Content Per Module (Standard Structure)

Each module contains:
- **Lesson HTML** (~400-600 words, diagrams, tables)
- **Quiz XML** (5-7 multiple choice questions with solutions)
- **Video** (YouTube embed — NVIDIA/HuggingFace/LeRobot public content)
- **Additional Resources** (3-5 links to docs/papers/tutorials)
- **Lab Exercise** (optional — hands-on for Course 2 & 3 tied to MarsGeo platform)
